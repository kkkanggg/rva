Artist Statement

As an IT student with a passion for both technology and visual expression, my work explores the intersection of digital tools and artistic creativity. Through each module, I sought to understand design not merely as a technical skill but as a language — one that communicates ideas, emotions, and cultural identity. From observational drawing to digital poster-making, every piece challenged me to see the world with greater intention and curiosity. I am especially drawn to how Filipino aesthetics can be woven into contemporary digital formats, bridging heritage and innovation. This portfolio reflects my growth as both a creator and a critical thinker, documenting the journey from beginner to a more confident digital artist who understands that every pixel, pattern, and composition carries meaning.

— Kate Angela Pasag
